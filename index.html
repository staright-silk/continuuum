PALESTINE BEFORE PARTITION

PRE - 1917

Palestine exists as a lived geography. Muslims, Christians, Jews live across cities and villages. Land ownership documented locally, communally, generationally. A place does not need permission to exist.

1917 — THE DECLARATION THAT ERASED A PEOPLE

Imperial Promise

The British Empire issues a declaration. A homeland is promised to Zionist leadership. The indigenous Palestinian majority is not consulted. Colonialism begins with a sentence. Britain | World War I | Mandate governance

1920–1947 — THE MANDATE YEARS

Administration as Preparation

British rule facilitates demographic engineering. Armed Zionist militias form and train. Palestinian revolts suppressed with collective punishment. Villages fined. Homes demolished. Leaders exiled. Resistance criminalized before dispossession is complete.

1947 — PARTITION WITHOUT CONSENT

Lines Drawn From Afar

The land is divided by international vote. The majority population loses most of the territory. Implementation backed by force, not agreement. A vote cannot overwrite reality.

1948 — NAKBA

The Catastrophe

Over 750,000 Palestinians expelled. 500+ villages destroyed or depopulated. Massacres documented by historians. Homes seized under "absentee" laws. Refugee camps expanding. New towns built on erased land.

1949–1966 — REFUGE WITHOUT RETURN

Exile Institutionalized

Refugees denied return. Borders sealed. Property confiscated permanently. Displacement becomes policy when time passes without justice.

1967 — OCCUPATION MULTIPLIES

Control as Daily Life

Gaza Strip and West Bank occupied. Military law imposed on civilians. Settlements expand, subsidized and protected. One land. Two legal systems.

1970s–1980s — NORMALIZATION OF CONTROL

Bureaucracy of Domination

Movement restricted. Water controlled. Land fragmented. Curfews. Raids. Administrative detention. Violence becomes routine when paperwork precedes it.

1987 — THE FIRST INTIFADA

Civil Resistance Meets Military Power

Popular uprising. Grassroots organizing. Children targeted with arrests. The world calls it unrest. Palestinians call it survival.

1993 — OSLO ACCORDS

Process Over Justice

Temporary agreement becomes permanent delay. Land carved into zones. Settlements expand faster. Peace without equality is management.

2000–2005 — SECOND INTIFADA

Repression Intensifies

Separation barrier constructed. Cities enclosed. Casualties escalate. Security becomes the language that excuses everything.

2007 — GAZA UNDER SIEGE

Containment

Air, land, sea blockade. Food, fuel, medicine restricted. Infrastructure collapses slowly. Most water undrinkable. Collective punishment is illegal.

2008–2022 — RECURRING MILITARY ASSAULTS

Operations With Different Names, Same Outcome

Thousands killed. Hospitals hit. Journalists targeted. Investigations stalled. When accountability never arrives, escalation is guaranteed.

2023–PRESENT — GENOCIDAL ESCALATION

Intent Made Visible

Mass displacement ordered. Civilian infrastructure destroyed. Entire families erased from records. Genocide defined by intent + action.

INTERNATIONAL RESPONSE

Selective Outrage

Resolutions blocked. Military aid continues. Media reframes asymmetry as "clashes." Neutrality in genocide is alignment.

THE PRESENT AS A RESULT, NOT AN ANOMALY

This did not begin suddenly. It was built deliberately. History does not repeat. It continues when unaddressed.
